Semester 2 expanded my technical foundation through programming, system design, computer architecture, statistics, ethics, and user-centered design. It helped me connect theory with practical assignments and small projects.

## Reflection

This semester helped me see that software development is more than writing programs. I learned to understand systems, users, data, hardware behavior, and ethical responsibilities as connected parts of computing work.
