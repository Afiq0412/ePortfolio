# 🤖 Semester 5

> An applied semester focused on intelligent systems, enterprise technology, database programming, communication, and scientific curiosity.

## ✨ Semester Focus

Semester 5 introduced more advanced and applied areas of computing, where I began connecting technical knowledge with real-world systems and organizational needs. Artificial Intelligence exposed me to intelligent problem-solving methods and helped me understand how systems can make decisions or support automation. Database Programming strengthened my practical skills in SQL, database scripts, stored procedures, and NoSQL work. Enterprise System and System Modelling showed me how large organizations use integrated systems to manage business processes and data. Professional Communication Skills 2 improved my ability to write proposals, pitch ideas, and communicate professionally, while Astronomy gave me a broader scientific perspective and reminded me that learning can extend beyond computing into curiosity about the universe.

## 📝 Reflection

Semester 5 helped me see computing in larger and more realistic contexts. I learned that technology is often used inside organizations to support operations, manage data, improve communication, and make better decisions. The combination of AI, database programming, and enterprise systems gave me a stronger understanding of how intelligent and data-driven solutions can be applied in real environments. I also improved my professional communication through proposal writing and pitching activities, which are important when explaining technical ideas to others. This semester made me more confident in handling applied computing tasks and helped prepare me for project environments where technical skills, business understanding, and communication must work together.
