# 📊 Semester 6

> A data-driven and project-focused semester where software development, business intelligence, high-performance processing, and data engineering came together.

## ✨ Semester Focus

Semester 6 was a more data-driven and project-focused semester where I learned how different computing areas can work together to create complete digital solutions. Application Development helped me understand how applications are planned, documented, and designed based on user and system needs. Business Intelligence showed me how raw data can be transformed into dashboards, reports, KPIs, and insights that support decision-making. High Performance Data Processing taught me to think about efficiency, scalability, workload handling, and the importance of performance when dealing with larger data tasks. Special Topic in Data Engineering exposed me to modern tools and workflows such as cloud platforms, Spark concepts, notebooks, data pipelines, and industry-style data projects.

## 📝 Reflection

Semester 6 gave me a more complete view of how software development and data engineering support real decisions in practical environments. I learned that building useful digital solutions requires more than knowing one tool; it requires planning, understanding users, preparing reliable data, processing information efficiently, and presenting results clearly. Through BI dashboards, data engineering activities, application documentation, and performance-focused reports, I became more confident in connecting technical work with business or industry needs. This semester helped me improve my ability to document projects, use modern data tools, explain insights, and understand how different technologies can support one complete solution. It was one of the semesters that made me feel more prepared for real-world data and software-related work.
