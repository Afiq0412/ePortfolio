# Business Intelligence

## Course Overview

Business Intelligence taught me how raw data can become meaningful insight through preparation, analysis, visualization, and reporting. The folder includes dashboard files, Alteryx workflows, datasets, lab exercises, and project materials.

## What I Gained

I gained practical experience in turning business data into useful information for decision-making. I practiced cleaning and preparing data, building workflows, creating dashboards, and presenting findings in a way that is easier for users to understand.

## Skills Practiced

| Area | Growth |
| --- | --- |
| Data preparation | Cleaning, combining, and shaping datasets for analysis |
| BI tools | Using Power BI and Alteryx for dashboards and workflows |
| Visualization | Designing reports that highlight KPIs and trends |
| Decision support | Connecting data findings to business questions |

## Reflection

This course showed me that BI is not only about charts. It is about asking the right questions, preparing reliable data, and presenting insights clearly. I became more confident in using BI tools to support practical business decisions.
